item: H-POL-1
date: 2026-04-11T19:21:05Z
summary: Keep live policy updates network-only and reject fs/proc drift
context: The work item required session-driven policy reload and replace during a running launch session while keeping filesystem mounts and proc allowlists restart-bound.
decision: Session control accepts inline Cedar source for `policy.replace`, but both `policy.reload` and `policy.replace` only swap the active engine when the effective fs and proc permissions stay unchanged. If either domain would change, the update is rejected with a structured restart-required result and the previous policy remains active.
rationale: This preserves one explicit runtime contract across SIGHUP and session API updates, avoids partially applying a policy that the container sandbox cannot honor live, and gives later CLI work a stable control-plane shape to target.
